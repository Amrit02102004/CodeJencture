VITMAS CLUB

•VIT Mathematical Association's student chapter established in 2017 is aimed to empower students by extending their boundaries of understanding and equipping them with the knowledge and skill to appreciate the most powerful tool in the world - Mathematics. We hope to achieve our aim of improving the understanding of mathematics among the student population of VIT through a program of research-based learning and change how Mathematics is taught and learned. In simpler terms, VITMAS seeks to rekindle students’ love for numbers with events and workshops to enliven mathematics for the strategies to understand probabilities and forecasts of industries and with an ambition to take mathematics beyond classrooms. QUIZ CLUB

The Quiz Club of VIT, Vellore, better known as DBQC, is one of the oldest clubs in VIT for the development and promotion of the most fun, competitive and intellectual sport— quizzing. We bring a vibrant quizzing culture wherever we go (and we do go to a lot of places, winning laurels) and have established ourselves as one of the most successful quiz clubs in India's quizzing circuit. We foster the fun nature of quizzing among college students, not restricting ourselves to the same old pattern of banal question-answering. We pack our quizzes with loads of interesting fundae so there's something for everyone! Some of our events include Quizzes of the Month, Summer of Quiz, Weekend Minis, and several themed quizzes that become our college fest's flagship events. Open to everyone, no holds barred, excellent quizzing and shenanigans is what we do!
